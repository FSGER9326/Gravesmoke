# v0.8 Vertical Slice Spec — Greyhook Fortress

## Goal

Turn Greyhook Fortress into the first complete barrier chapter. The player should be able to reach Greyhook, discover or earn a route inside, enter through a chosen approach, navigate interior pressure, reach the sealed prisoner, decide the prisoner's fate, and leave with consequences.

## Chapter promise

```text
Get through Greyhook Fortress.
Find the sealed prisoner.
Decide what the prisoner becomes: witness, burden, corpse, or currency.
Escape the consequences.
```

## Required route families

### Supply escort

- Starts from Free Spears / Muster Field.
- Lets the party keep weapons.
- Raises scrutiny in the outer yard.
- Best for mercenary, martial, and honorable builds.

### Quartermaster blackmail

- Starts from Quartermaster's Tent.
- Uses missing supplies, mistress records, or Red Ledger pressure.
- Opens lower-cell service access.
- High future enemy risk.

### Infirmary route

- Starts from Chapel / Fortress Infirmary.
- Restricts visible weapons.
- Opens medical records, nameless prisoner clue, and quieter prisoner contact.
- Best for faith, mercy, healer, or Synod-aligned parties.

### Forged transfer

- Starts from Greyhook Gate / papers route.
- High inspection risk.
- Can fail forward into detention or captured-inside route.
- Best for sly, criminal, and high-Guile parties.

### Captured-inside route

- Starts through failed escape, staged arrest, or deliberate surrender.
- Costs gear, wounds, or time.
- Places the party near lower cells.
- Should never be a hard game over.

### Escape route

- Opens when Greyhook Alert rises.
- Can be discovered by scouting, camp planning, Mara/Nell, or lockdown.
- Used to survive high-alert exits.

## Alert ladder

```text
0–1 routine
2–3 tightened searches
4 lockdown
5+ forced escape / capture pressure
```

## Alert effects

### Routine

- Normal route checks.
- Gate and yard scenes available.

### Tightened searches

- Forged papers risk increases.
- Visible weapons create suspicion.
- Patrol confrontation can appear.

### Lockdown

- Some exits become blocked or risky.
- Escape route lead appears.
- Starting enemy can frame the player.

### Forced escape / capture

- Player must escape, surrender, or use a special route.
- Failure forwards into lower-cell/capture state.

## Sealed prisoner choices

### Question

- Gain testimony.
- Improves truth/mercy/faction paths.
- Does not solve extraction.

### Extract

- Gain living witness.
- Raises alert.
- Requires key, paper, infirmary cover, violent distraction, or high Guile.

### Silence

- Gain blackmail / Red Ledger value.
- Major morale damage for merciful companions.
- Crime and future haunting hooks.

### Trade

- Gain faction reward.
- Creates betrayal record.
- Closes some trust paths.

## Companion reaction targets

- Mara: respects clean extraction and scout planning.
- Hadrik: approves soldierly routes and honorable prisoner handling.
- Nell: supports clever exits but dislikes betraying locked-door trust.
- Sarn: cares about paper trails and real receipts.
- Ilyra/Cael: oppose witness murder and approve humane questioning.
- Brig: approves silencing and violent distraction.
- Miren: reacts to dead-name and Crown-of-Ash occult implications.

## v0.8 acceptance criteria

- A new player can complete the Ledger chapter and naturally unlock Greyhook.
- Greyhook has at least four viable entry approaches.
- Failed entry or extraction produces a playable captured/cell route.
- Alert changes available scenes or exit risk.
- The prisoner can be questioned, extracted, silenced, or traded.
- The chapter can resolve and produce a visible aftermath card.
- Companion reaction and morale are visible after prisoner fate.
- Game data passes `npm run validate`.
- Android debug workflow attempts to build an APK artifact.
